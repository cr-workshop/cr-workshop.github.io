About

Cross-reality (CR) applications transition between different points on Milgramâ€™s reality-virtuality continuum. CR implies the concurrent or sequential usage of multiple points on the continuum. The goal of the workshop is to discuss benefits and challenges in designing, developing, and using CR applications. One objective is to create a research agenda for cross-reality systems, with a focus on software and usability topics.
